The Battle of the Bands will be held on Wednesday, April 12, 2017 at the Loft.

APPLICATIONS FOR THIS YEAR’S BATTLE OF THE BANDS IS NOW CLOSED. We are currently preparing the voting website in which all bands will be voted upon to determine who will move on to the Battle of the Bands.

The voting period will be February 27 through March 17. Once voting closes, the top 3 bands plus the "ASCE Staff Pick" will be announced to perform at the Battle of the Bands on April 12. Judges will determine the winner at the Battle of the Bands event. This year’s winning band will win a 30 minute opening set on the MAIN STAGE at the Sun God Festival 2017!

If you have any questions please contact ascefestivals@ucsd.edu
